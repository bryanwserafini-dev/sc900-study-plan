📌 Your Advantage — Read Before You Begin Your CC and Security+ mean Domains 1 concepts (Zero Trust, CIA Triad, encryption, authentication types) are already familiar territory. Treat those days as a Microsoft-flavored vocabulary refresh, not from-scratch learning. Shift your energy to Domains 2–4 where Microsoft's specific product ecosystem (Entra, Defender, Purview, Sentinel) is the actual challenge. You don't need to configure anything — this exam asks "what is it and why would you use it?"
